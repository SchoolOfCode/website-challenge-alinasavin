My story

I grew up in Birmingham before making my way to Robin Hood’s valley, where I studied Fashion Business at Nottingham Trent University, however, being a passionate environmentalist made me leave this career path and follow a different path.

I have taken on several different jobs throughout my life: I have been in the modelling industry since I was 20, but also always enjoyed working with people so I have also taken on a series of different costumer facing roles. I flew to France in 2018 and learned a bit of French whilst working as a cleaner for several months. Coming back to UK I began a career in retail: working roles as a bra fitter, visual merchandiser and finally as a department store concession supervisor.

I quit my job and moved back to Birmingham to be close to family in March last year. With a desire and passion for the tech industry, I have been teaching myself web design, UX design and digital marketing through several online courses since December 2020, before deciding to learn how to code and become a School of Code Bootcamper!

Just like fashion, which is an art used by people daily and affects their everyday life, so is code! You can create something and people interact with, use, touch and work with it. This is an amazing feeling! All I want to do is to keep learning as much as I can and see where this journey takes me.

I’m a designer, craftswoman, a painter, and a lifetime student.

I’m an optimist. I’m a feminist. I’m an environmentalist. And I will be more things yet to come
