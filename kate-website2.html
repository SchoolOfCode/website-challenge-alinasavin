My story

I grew up in Birmingham before making it to Robin Hood’s valley. I spent 2 years studying Fashion Business at Nottingham Trent University. In love with arts I have been in the modelling industry since I was 20. Being a strong and passionate environmentalist made me leave this and follow a different path.

I always enjoyed working with people so I decided to take a series of different costumer facing roles. I flew to France in 2018. I learned French ‘ sur place’ whilst working as a cleaner for several months.. Coming back to UK I carried on with my career in retail as visual merchandiser. I quit my job as department store supervisor to reflect what I wanted to do next.

I moved back to Birmingham to be close to family in March last year. With a desire and passion into tech industry I have been teaching myself how to code through a couple of online courses since December 2020, before I became a School of Code Bootcamper. Just like fashion, which is an art used by people daily and affects their everyday life, so is code. You can create something and people interact with, use, touch and work with. This is an amazing feeling! All I want to do is to keep learning as much as I can and see where this journey takes me.
